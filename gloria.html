Gloria Arroyo visits tombs of parents-in-law at Manila North Cemetery
Harlene Delgado, ABS-CBN News
Published Nov 02, 2025 07:16 PM PHT
Former president and now Pampanga 2nd District Rep. Gloria Arroyo at the Manila North Cemetery. Photo courtesy of Daniel Tan

MANILA -- Former president and now Pampanga 2nd District Rep. Gloria Arroyo visited the tombs of her parents-in-law at the Manila North Cemetery on All Souls' Day, November 2.

In photos provided by the cemetery's Director Daniel Tan, the former chief executive can be see with former First Gentleman Jose Miguel "Mike" Arroyo, 79 and son former Pampanga Representative Mikey Arroyo.

The former president visited the tomb of her parents, former President Diosdado Macapagal and former First Lady Eva Macaraig Macapagal on All Saints' Day, November 1, based on a Facebook post of Mikey.
