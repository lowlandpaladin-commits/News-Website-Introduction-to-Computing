Tennis: Alex Eala breaks into Top 50 of WTA rankings
ABS-CBN News
Published Nov 03, 2025 01:19 PM PHT | Updated Nov 03, 2025 08:27 PM PHT
Philippines' Alexandra Eala reacts as she plays against Czech Republic's Barbora Krejcikova during their women's singles first round tennis match on the second day of the 2025 Wimbledon Championships at The All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon, southwest London, on July 1, 2025. Glyn Kirk, AFP/File

Philippines' Alexandra Eala reacts as she plays against Czech Republic's Barbora Krejcikova during their women's singles first round tennis match on the second day of the 2025 Wimbledon Championships at The All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon, southwest London, on July 1, 2025. Glyn Kirk, AFP/File MANILA -- Filipina tennis star Alex Eala reached a new career-high on Monday, rising to No. 50 in the latest edition of the WTA rankings.

It's one spot higher than her previous ranking, as the Filipina improved after making it to the round-of-16 of the Hong Kong Open.

Eala's campaign in Hong Kong ended at the hands of eventual champion Victoria Mboko of Canada.

After her stint, Eala said her 2025 has been "really amazing," as she rose through the ranks steadily after her breakthrough in the Miami Open in March.

"The target I had to end the year was top 100, and that was kinda like pushing it for me, and we passed that by a milestone, so I’m so happy," said Eala.

Up next for Eala is the Southeast Asian Games in Thailand in December.
