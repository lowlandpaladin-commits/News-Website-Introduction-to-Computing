Undas 2025: Zsa Zsa Padilla, Eric Quizon remember Dolphy
Anna Cerezo, ABS-CBN News
Published Nov 02, 2025 02:58 PM PHT
(From left) Eric Quizon and ZsaZsa Padilla visit the grave of the late Comedy King Dolphy. Photos from Quzion and Padilla's respective Instagram accounts Singer Zsa Zsa Padilla and actor Eric Quizon paid tribute to the late screen icon Dolphy, hailed as the country's King of Comedy, as Filipinos marked Undas.

As she has lovingly done since his passing, Padilla once again made sure to remember her late partner on All Saints’ Day.

Padilla visited Dolphy's grave at the Heritage Park in Taguig City to honor his memory on Saturday.

“Eternal rest grant unto them, O Lord, and let perpetual light shine upon them. May their souls and all the souls of the faithful departed, through the mercy of God, rest in peace. Amen,” she captioned the series of photos of his grave she posted on her Instagram.

Meanwhile, Quizon paid an early visit to the grave of his father ahead of the All Souls’ Day observance.

In his Instagram post on Friday, Quizon shared a photo from Heritage Park in Taguig City, where his father is laid to rest.

“I decided to come earlier before it gets busy at the Heritage Park. Said a little prayer for my Dad in commemoration of All Souls’ Day. Surprised to see a wreath of flowers from the city of Manila! With our gratitude to Mayor,” he wrote.

Born Rodolfo Vera Quizon Sr., Dolphy died in July 2012 due to chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. He was 83.
