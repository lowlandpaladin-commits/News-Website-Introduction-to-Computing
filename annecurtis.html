Anne Curtis’ version of “GOLDEN” gets standing ovation at ASAP Vancouver
ABS-CBN Studios
Published Nov 02, 2025 01:51 PM PHT | Updated Nov 03, 2025 09:15 AM PHT
Straight from Stanley Park, “ASAP in Vancouver’s” gregarious hosts Darren Espanto, Alexa Ilacad, Belle Mariano, Robi Domingo, and Edward Barber introduced Anne Curtis’ highly anticipated rendition of the super trending song “Golden.” She indeed hyped everyone up as she belted it live, even repeating the chorus twice due to insistent public demand, making sure that the next one was higher than the previous one. And this performance unbelievably earned a standing ovation from the spectators!

Anne then made a curtsy as soon as she finished her number and asked everyone how her performance was. She went on to apologize to those manning the technical booth and express how happy she was to finally be able to sing “Golden” live. While she was delivering the rest of her speech, the crowd demanded for her to sing more, but Anne laughingly told them, “hindi ko na kaya!”
