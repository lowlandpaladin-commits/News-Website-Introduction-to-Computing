On authenticity: Actress Marian Rivera shares thoughts on integrity, transparency with Nuwhite
Philstar.com
October 30, 2025 | 6:28pm
MANILA, Philippines — Marian Rivera has once again been celebrated for her beauty, authenticity, transparency and inner glow as a woman during the formal signing event of her contract renewal with food supplement and glutathione brand Nuwhite in Makati City.

Such transparency of the Kapuso Primetime Queen has been applauded by the fans and press members alike, an attribute aligned with Nuwhite’s core values as a brand.

A food supplement known for its formulation of pure and authentic 200 milligrams of S-Acetyl, the most premium kind of glutathione, Nuwhite is a beauty and wellness capsule that solves more than just your skin issues. It is an effective antioxidant that helps regulate your rest as you can get quality sleep, as well as assists you with managing stress.

S-Acetyl is most effective when it is given at 200 milligrams per capsule, a transparency in formulation that Nuwhite has been proud of for years. This authenticity in its formula has been the backbone of the year-long campaign of Nuwhite dubbed as “Glow for 200,” a series of brand activities that celebrate women who glow from within because of their integrity and inner beauty— from Nuwhite’s #WhatMakesYouGlow visual art expression in March, to its monthly activation of putting the spotlight on women who glow from within with their integrity and inner beauty.

“Marian Rivera stands for that same integrity and transparency that Nuwhite has been revered for. Nuwhite’s ‘Glow for 200’ campaign that kicked off earlier this year has celebrated the integrity, credibility, brilliance and inner peace of women — core values that define not just our brand, but the women who resonate with Nuwhite’s honest formulation as a beauty and wellness product,” Maureen Estrada, Nuwhite founder and CEO, shared in a press statement.

While other glutathione brands would promise formulations that are not completely honest and transparent, Nuwhite guarantees top-notch wellness benefits that are value for money for its clientele. Nuwhite has the right dosage of the best kind of glutathione in the market, backed by science proven to deliver maximum skincare, wellness and health benefits.

Such honesty of the brand resonates well with the transparency, realness and inner glow of renowned Filipina actress Marian Rivera.

The actress shared her thoughts on inner beauty rooted in authenticity and beautiful glow caused by one’s realness and transparency.

"Kapag sinabi nating glowing, mahalaga sa akin ang glowing from inside and out. So hindi lang dahil maganda sa labas, maganda na. Mas maganda pa rin 'yung good combination na maganda ka na sa labas, pero mas maganda kung ano nasa kalooban,” Marian said during the press conference of her contract renewal with Nuwhite.

During the contract-signing event, the press members got to see a peek at the latest photo shoot of the brand ambassador for upcoming campaigns and collaborations.

"Of course, proud ako sa Nuwhite kasi maganda talaga 'yung produkto nila, very transparent talaga sila. Kung ano ang nagagawa ng produkto, at ano ba talaga 'yung ingredients na inilagay nila, never nagtatago ang Nuwhite. Lagi nagsasabi ng totoo about their formulation, and ano ang ginagawa nito to your skin, at ang nagagawa ng pag-glow sa’yo. So para sakin, magandang bagay 'yung pagiging transparent nila at totoo, at kung ano 'yung sinasabi ng Nuwhite about sa ingredients and formulation, 'yun lang talaga 'yung totoo na meron ang Nuwhite,” she added.

“Bilang tao, at bilang artista, kung ano man ang position ko sa buhay, kapag meron ka kasing tinatago, ang hirap. At ang hirap magpanggap. Kapag totoo ka, lahat magfo-follow na ‘yan, at wala kang dapat itago. So para sakin, mahalaga talaga kapag totoo ka sa sarili mo, kasi paano magiging smooth at magiging maayos ang lahat kung 'di ka transparent? Makikita at makikita ng mga tao ‘yan. So para sakin, mas maganda pa rin na authentic ka, at transparent ka bilang tao. Wala kang tinatago kasi 'yun nga 'yung sinasabi ko, ang pagiging totoo, 'yan ang pinaka powerful na hawak mo na, you know, ikaw mismo sa sarili mo, you love yourself and who you are. And everything else will follow,” Marian concluded.

“Every capsule of Nuwhite contains 200 milligrams of S-Acetyl, and it is at the heart of every Nuwhite capsule. It is a very powerful antioxidant, as it’s not only skin brightening, but it is for overall wellness, because it helps with managing stress, detoxifying your liver. It can energize you, regulate your sleep, and of course, we will really glow from within,” the Nuwhite CEO said during the event, wrapping up the occasion.

The contract-signing event saw the celebration of Nuwhite’s four-year relationship with the actress, while signaling the latest phase of their partnership with a new two-year contract for Marian as the brand’s ambassador.
